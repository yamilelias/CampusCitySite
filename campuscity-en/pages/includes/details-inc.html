Details

The urban development plan includes: mixed-use community center, commerce, services, entertainment, sports and recreation.


Concept

The project benefits around 65 thousand families, ie 250 thousand inhabitants and more than 25 thousand students from different educational institutions and has the following elements:

  • Living
    • Rent & Sale of departments
  • Educative Center
    • Space dedicated to educational institutions at university level
    • Bookstores & Stationery
  • Public space and mobility
    • Square
    • Parks
    • Walkways and bikeways
  • Commerce
    • Anchors: Supermarkets, a wholesale computer and office supplies, Movies
    • Commercial Plaza (indoor and outdoor gallery premises)
    • Stores daily consumption
  • Services
    • Private and public offices
    • Banks
    • Gas station
  • Entertainment & Health
    • Style Life Center: restaurants, bars, boutiques, shops,
    • Movie Theater
    • Gym
    • Private-clinical and laboratory clinics
    • Restaurants-franchises
